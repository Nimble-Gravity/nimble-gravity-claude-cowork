# Axos Cowork — Change-Management Plan (compressed engagement)

A lean OCM plan sized to the **actual engagement window**, not a generic 8-week rollout.
This is the off-site companion to the in-portal change pieces (Why Cowork, Rules of the
Road, the acknowledgment gate). Hand it to the Axos sponsor and program owner.

## Engagement shape

- **Format:** four 2-hour, facilitator-led workshops (one per module) = 8 hours of class time.
- **Engagement length:** **2–4 weeks.**
- **Total OCM envelope:** **~6 weeks max** — **1 week before** (awareness + readiness) +
  the engagement + **1 week after** (recognition + handoff).
- Because reinforcement time is short, the strategy is: **front-load Awareness/Desire before
  the engagement, and hand off sustainment to managers + champions in the week after.**

Two viable cadences (pick with the sponsor):

| | Cadence | Envelope | Trade-off |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Option A — Intensive** | 2 workshops/week over 2 weeks | ~4 weeks total | Tightest; less homework/adoption time between sessions. Best when calendars are hard to hold. |
| **Option B — Weekly (recommended if possible)** | 1 workshop/week over ~4 weeks | ~6 weeks total | More room for homework, reinforcement, and adoption to take hold between sessions. |

## Roles (RACI-lite)

| Role | Who | Owns |
|---|---|---|
| **Executive sponsor** | Axos leader | Visible "why," 4 touchpoints, recognition |
| **Program owner** | Nimble Gravity | The plan, comms, measurement, readout |
| **Facilitator** | Nimble Gravity | Delivery, demos, office hours |
| **Managers** | Axos | Calendar time, encouragement, reinforcement |
| **Champions** | 2–3 Axos participants | Peer help, success stories, sustainment |

## 1. Sponsor roadmap (4 touchpoints — realistic for an exec)

1. **T‑1 week — Kickoff email.** The "why," the ask, the dates; names it a priority. Links Why Cowork + Rules of the Road.
2. **Workshop 1 — Open live (5 min)** (or a 60-sec recorded intro). Presence at the start signals it matters.
3. **Mid (after Workshop 2) — Reinforcement note.** "Great momentum — here's an early win." 3 sentences.
4. **T+1 week — Recognition + path forward.** Thanks the cohort, names champions and office hours, points to the certificate.

## 2. Communications calendar (~6-week envelope)

Keyed to milestones so it works at either cadence. ~11 touchpoints — lean but covers Awareness → Reinforcement.

| When | Audience | Channel | Message / purpose | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **T‑1 wk (Mon)** | Participants + managers | Email | **Sponsor kickoff** — why, the ask, dates, links to Why Cowork + Rules of the Road | Sponsor (draft: Program owner) |
| **T‑1 wk (Tue)** | Managers | Email / 15-min huddle | **Manager briefing** — protect calendar time, encourage real tasks, nominate champions | Program owner |
| **T‑1 wk (Thu)** | Participants | Email / Teams | **Get ready** — confirm Cowork access, do pre-work, acknowledge Rules of the Road, take the maturity poll | Facilitator |
| **Day before each WS** | Participants | Teams / calendar | **Reminder + pre-work nudge** — "Tomorrow: Workshop N; bring [pre-work]" | Facilitator |
| **Day after each WS** | Participants | Email / Teams | **Recap + homework + one quick win** from the room | Facilitator |
| **After WS 2** | Participants + managers | Email / Teams | **Sponsor/champion reinforcement** — early wins, momentum | Sponsor / Champions |
| **T+1 wk (Mon)** | Participants + sponsor + managers | Email / Teams | **Close-out + recognition** — thanks, completion + certificate prompt, what's next | Sponsor + Program owner |
| **T+1 wk (Wed)** | Managers | Email | **Sustainment note** — keep reinforcing; here's how | Program owner |
| **T+1 wk (Fri)** | Sponsor + IT | 1-page doc / 30-min readout | **Engagement readout** — attendance, completion, pre/post maturity, early adoption, next steps | Program owner |
| **T+30 (optional)** | Participants | Microsoft Form + 1 office hour | **30-day pulse** — still using it? what's blocking? | Champions |

## 3. Measurement plan (lean — don't over-instrument a short engagement)

- **Baseline (T‑1 / Workshop 1 open):** the **maturity poll** (already in the site) + one confidence question.
- **Leading indicators (during):** attendance per workshop, pre-work completion, Rules-of-the-Road acknowledgment rate, quizzes passed, skills shipped (Workshop 3).
- **Lagging indicators (T+1, T+30):** **post maturity poll** (re-measure for a before/after), self-reported active use, deliverables produced, time saved (self-report), NPS.
- **Example targets (set with the sponsor):** ≥80% attend all four · ≥75% pass all four quizzes · average **+1 maturity level** · ≥1 skill shipped per person · ≥50% still using at 30 days.
- **Instruments:** the maturity poll (pre/post) and quizzes are on the site; add a **3-question post-session Microsoft Form** (reaction/NPS) and a **30-day pulse Form**; pull active usage from the admin dashboard / Analytics API if licensed.
- **Readout:** a **one-page engagement readout** to sponsor + IT at T+1. Note: the site is client-only (localStorage), so cohort numbers are **aggregated manually by the facilitator** until a real backend/LMS exists.

## 4. Reinforcement in a short window (front-load it)

- **Office hours:** 2–3 during the engagement + 1 in the post week.
- **Champions:** spot them in Workshops 2–3, brief them in Workshop 4, hand off in the post week.
- **Success stories:** capture 2–3 during; publish in the close-out comms.
- **Embed:** point people to the resource library + #cowork channel as the ongoing home; one 30-day check-in keeps it alive past the formal close.

## 5. Pre-flight checklist (this engagement)

- ☐ Sponsor named; kickoff email drafted
- ☐ **Rules of the Road signed off by Legal / Compliance / InfoSec** (blocking)
- ☐ Licenses + Cowork access provisioned and verified for the cohort
- ☐ Dates locked; participant + manager calendars held
- ☐ Managers briefed; 2–3 champions provisionally identified
- ☐ Facilitator ready; demos rehearsed (see facilitator guide)
- ☐ Pre-work page + acknowledgment gate live; maturity-poll baseline ready
- ☐ Post-session Form + 30-day pulse built; readout template ready
